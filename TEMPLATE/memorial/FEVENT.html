International Exhibition of Book & Publishing

The Casablanca International Book Fair is a book fair held annually at the International Fair in Casablanca, Morocco. This exhibition is considered one of the largest cultural festivals in the Kingdom of Morocco, and it is held in February every year for ten days in the economic capital, under the auspices of King Mohammed VI, under the supervision of the Ministry of Culture, Youth and Sports, and organized in cooperation with the Moroccan Agency for the Development of Investments and Exports and the Exhibition Office. The exhibition area is more than 20,000 square meters, which makes it one of the most important cultural gatherings that bring together various cultural actors with their various intellectual and literary orientations, including authors, painters, publishers and artists, in addition to visitors to the exhibition of different groups and ages.

International Exhibition of Publishing and Books, SIEL is a unique platform which will facilitate the display of latest trends and innovations of Book Publishing. It is characterized as an entrusted place for Printing & Publishing sector.
